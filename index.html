About SELF.

SELF. is a brand that publishes work.

Its first published work is Self Burger and Self Slice — a virtual restaurant, delivered by Uber Eats out of partner kitchens across the United States. Every SKU is a psychological archetype mapped to your fandoms: zodiac, MBTI, anime archetypes, and D&D-style classes. Each order arrives with a small card, a case number, and a 100-word reading by our fictional Guest Analyst, Dr. Vera Monart.

What stays constant is one gesture: the customer picks a piece of themselves off a menu and eats it in public.
