Frequently Asked

What is SELF.?

SELF. is a virtual restaurant brand family. Its first two brands — Self Burger and Self Slice — are delivered by Uber Eats out of partner kitchens across the United States. Every SKU is a psychological archetype (The Ego, The Shadow, The Inner Child, The Mother, The Rebel, The Impostor, The Perfectionist), mapped to your favorite fandoms: zodiac, MBTI, anime archetypes, and D&D-style classes.

How do I take the quiz?

Visit theselfburger.com/quiz. Three questions. 30 seconds. You get your archetype + a working Uber Eats deep-link that pre-loads the recommended burger.

What do the fandom overlays mean?

Every archetype maps to 6 fandom layers: SELF. Element (our original character type), Zodiac sign, MBTI 16-personality type, anime archetype, RPG class, and an emoji signature. When you take the quiz, the result screen shows all six — so you can screenshot and share whichever layer speaks to your friend group.
